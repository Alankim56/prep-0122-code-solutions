Spiderman

Spiderman 1

Peter Parker gets bit by a radioactive spider that enables him to obtain the powers of an enhanced spider. He discovers that he can climb walls, emit webs from his hands,and has enhanced reflexes. Through utlization of his newfound powers, Peter Parker goes against a villan dubbed, the Green Goblin, and ultimately proves victorious

Spiderman 2

In this movie, Peter Parker begins to lose his powers and he begins to lose faith in himself. By questioning in his belief about the purpose of Spiderman, his powers begin to subside and ultimately return him to a normal human being. However, as his close loved ones begin to confront danger, Peter Parker reacquires his powers as he desires to save them from peril

Spiderman 3

In this movie, Peter Parker makes terms with his friend, Harry Osborn. Together, they unite to fight against Venom and Sandman.
